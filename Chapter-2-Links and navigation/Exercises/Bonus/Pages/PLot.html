Anne with an E

Storyline:

The series is based on a novel called ”Anne of the Green Gables” written by the Canadian author Lucy Maud. The series talks about Anne.
who is a an unconventional girl who faces the hardships of life and challenges while seeking love, acceptance, and finding a place for her in the world.

The story is located in Prince Edward Island. The little orphaned Anne lived a hard childhood in shelters and then worked as a servant,
After that, a mistake led her to be sent by to live with the two siblings “Mathew and Marilla Cuthbert”.
Over time, little Anne changed the lives of the two siblings and the whole village with her unique spirit, high intelligence and vivid imagination through a series of adventures.

Genre:Drama, Family, Adventure

Original languge:English
Seasons:3 Seasons(27 episodes)

About the author

Lucy Maud Montgomery (born Nov. 30, 1874, Clifton, died April 24, 1942, Toronto) Canadian regional romantic novelist, best known for Anne of Green Gables (1908).
A sentimentalized but often charming story of a spirited, unconventional orphan girl who finds a home with an elderly couple.
The book drew on the author’s own girlhood experiences and on the rural life and traditions of Prince Edward Island.
Earlier a journalist and schoolteacher, she achieved international success with both adults and children after the publication of Anne.
Six sequels, carrying Anne from girlhood to motherhood, were less successful.
