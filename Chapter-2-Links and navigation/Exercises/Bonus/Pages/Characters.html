Characters of Anne with an E

Main Characters

Anne Shirley Cuthbert

Anne Cordelia Shirley-Cuthbert is the titular protagonist of Anne. She is the orphaned daughter of Walter Shirley and Bertha Shirley, and the adoptive daughter of Marilla and Matthew Cuthbert. She is portrayed by Amybeth McNulty.

Matthew Cuthbert

Matthew Cuthbert is one of the main characters of Anne. He is portrayed by R.H. Thomson.

Marilla Cuthbert

Marilla Cuthbert is one of the main characters of Anne. She is portrayed by Geraldine James.

Gilbert Blythe

Mr. Phillips

Mr. Phillips was a teacher at the Avonlea school who had a secret relationship with Prissy Andrews. He is a recurring character in Anne and was portrayed by Stephen Tracey. After a brief engagement, he was left by Prissy at the altar and left town.

Minnie May Barry

Minnie May Barry is a minor character in Anne. She is the younger sister of Diana Barry, the daughter of William and Eliza Barry and the great-niece of Josephine Barry.

Prissy Andrews

Priscilla 'Prissy' Andrews is a minor character in the Canadian TV series, "Anne", although her character is further developed in the second season of the series. Prissy attends school with Anne, but looks and acts a lot older, as she developed a romantic relationship with Mr. Phillips, the teacher at their school.
