Trivia

The title of each episode in season 1 is a quote from the 1847 novel 'Jane Eyre' written by Charlotte Brontë.
The title of each episode in season 2 is a quote from the 1871 novel 'Middlemarch' written by George Eliot.
The title of each episode in season 3 is a quote from the 1818 novel 'Frankenstein' written by Mary Shelley

Spoiler Alert !!

Anne in school

When Anne enters school, she’s immediately ostracized because she’s an orphan. Diana Barry tries to be her friend but succumbs to peer pressure from classmates like Josie Pye. Although Diana doesn’t mistreat Anne as the others do, she becomes a silent bystander, which in modern viewers’ eyes makes her as guilty as the bullies. Meanwhile, Anne’s teacher Mr. Phillips is borderline sadistic. He doesn’t acknowledge or respect Anne’s academic prowess. Instead, he regularly humiliates her in front of the class.

Anne & Gilbert

As for the show’s romantic subplot, Gilbert begins the finale by ending his engagement to Winnie, much to her dismay and confusion.He then confesses his love to Anne in a letter, but she’s so furious upon receiving it that she rips it intro shreds, allowing her to leave for Queens without any emotional hang-ups. But when Diana learns that Gilbert has called off his engagement, she tells him about the letter Anne wrote. Gilbert rushes to Anne’s new house, and they agree to write each other from a distance
